# Firebase — from Firebase Console → Project Settings → Your Apps
VITE_FIREBASE_API_KEY=AIza...
VITE_FIREBASE_AUTH_DOMAIN=cashflowtracker-xyz.firebaseapp.com
VITE_FIREBASE_PROJECT_ID=cashflowtracker-xyz
VITE_FIREBASE_APP_ID=1:123456:web:abc...

# No ANTHROPIC_API_KEY here — users bring their own in Settings
⚡ VITE_ prefix makes vars available in the browser bundle. Firebase public config is safe to expose — it's not a secret (security comes from Firestore rules + Auth, not from hiding these keys).
